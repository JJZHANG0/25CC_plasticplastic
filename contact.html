Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What industries can benefit from AquaPure technology?

Our technology is ideal for aquaculture facilities (fish, shrimp, shellfish farming), nearshore water management, coastal industrial operations, marine research institutions, and municipal water quality programs.

How long does installation take?

Standard installation of a 1-1.5 m³ unit typically takes 2-3 days, including system integration, calibration, and operator training. Custom large-scale deployments may require 1-2 weeks depending on site conditions.

What maintenance is required?

Minimal maintenance is needed—quarterly inspection of ultrasonic transducers, bi-annual hydrocyclone cleaning, and annual system calibration. Our intelligent monitoring system alerts you to any maintenance needs automatically.

Can the system be scaled for larger operations?

Absolutely. Our modular design allows multiple units to operate in parallel, scaling from small pilot installations to industrial-scale deployments processing hundreds of cubic meters per day.

What is the expected ROI?

Most aquaculture operations achieve ROI within 18-24 months through reduced contamination losses, premium product pricing, and elimination of batch rejections. Municipal projects see long-term value through ecosystem protection and regulatory compliance.

Do you offer research partnerships?

Yes! We actively collaborate with universities, research institutes, and environmental organizations. Contact us to discuss joint research projects, pilot studies, or technology validation programs.
