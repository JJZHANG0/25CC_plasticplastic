About AquaPure

Pioneering environmental technology for a cleaner planet

Our Mission

Enhancing microplastic governance capacity to protect ecosystems and human health through intelligent, sustainable water purification solutions.

We are committed to developing breakthrough technologies that address the urgent environmental crisis of microplastic pollution, ensuring cleaner water for aquaculture, marine ecosystems, and coastal communities worldwide.

Our Vision

Zero microplastic discharge for future aquaculture and coastal habitats, creating a world where water systems thrive free from plastic contamination.

We envision a future where advanced environmental technology becomes the standard for water management, protecting biodiversity, ensuring food safety, and preserving the health of our oceans for generations to come.

The Challenge We're Solving

Confronting the global microplastic crisis with innovative engineering

01

Ecosystem Contamination

Microplastics accumulate in aquaculture facilities and nearshore waters, disrupting marine ecosystems, harming aquatic life, and entering the food chain through bioaccumulation.

02

Human Health Risks

Contaminated seafood introduces microplastics and associated toxins into human diets, posing significant health risks including inflammation, oxidative stress, and potential carcinogenic effects.

03

Inefficient Removal Methods

Traditional filtration systems fail to capture particles smaller than 100 micrometers, are energy-intensive, require frequent maintenance, and cannot provide proactive early-stage prevention.

04

Economic Impact

Aquaculture industries face product quality degradation, regulatory compliance challenges, and market reputation damage due to microplastic contamination in their water systems.

Our Story

Born from a shared passion for environmental engineering and marine conservation, AquaPure Innovation emerged to tackle one of the 21st century's most insidious pollutants.

Our founding team of environmental engineers, marine scientists, and technology innovators witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of microplastic pollution on coastal ecosystems and aquaculture operations. We recognized that existing solutions were reactive, inefficient, and inadequate for the scale of the problem.

This realization sparked years of intensive research and development, combining ultrasonic technology, fluid dynamics, and intelligent automation to create a revolutionary approach to microplastic removal. Our breakthrough came when we successfully integrated ultrasonic pre-treatment with hydrocyclone separation, achieving unprecedented 96% removal efficiency.

Today, AquaPure stands at the forefront of environmental water purification, offering aquaculture facilities and coastal water management systems a proactive, energy-efficient, and scientifically proven solution to microplastic contamination.
