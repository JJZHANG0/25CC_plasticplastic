The Microplastic Crisis

Microplastics have become one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time, infiltrating aquatic ecosystems and threatening marine life, human health, and food security.

14M tons of plastic enter oceans annually
83% of seafood contains microplastics
5.25T plastic particles floating in oceans

Traditional filtration methods are inefficient, energy-intensive, and fail to capture particles smaller than 100 micrometers. Aquaculture facilities and nearshore waters urgently need innovative, proactive solutions.
